POSITIVE IMPACTS OF TECHNOLOGY ON WATCH INDUSTRY

Implementation of technology in watch industry has been a huge success for watch manufacturers. The incorporation of technology has simplified complex tasks, allowing them to be approached in a more efficient manner. Recently, the watch industry has been significantly influenced by advancements and the implementation of modern technology. The bridge between technology has given a positive impact in watch industry.

The emergence of smartwatches has transformed a simple or traditional watch into a multifunctional tech device, equipped with features such as sleep tracking, heart rate monitoring, various apps, Bluetooth calling, notification alerts, step counting, and pulse oximeter functions, representing a major upgrade compared to traditional watches. The rise of e-commerce has enabled brands to engage in international watch trade directly with consumers. This shift eliminates intermediaries between watch companies and customers. These developments have positively impacted the watch industry.

In conclusion, the watch industry has been benefited from the application of technology in a variety of areas which includes branding, e-commerce, advancements in watch design, production, and productivity, manufacturers have greatly profited with these impacts.
