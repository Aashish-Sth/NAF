Blend of Technology within the watch industry is increasing day by day due to a high consumer demand. Ecommerce website and it’s use has been significantly high due to the convenience provided by the technology. A consumer can choose his/her watch models based on their preferences. Due to ecommerce international trading of watches can be done by a brand directly with its customers. This also excludes middleman's between a watch company and a customer.

Feedback from customers have increased which has made a brand high in customer satisfaction becasue such feedbacks are being implemented by multiple brands. Tech giants such as Apple, Google, Samsung are innovating and researching a lot for a perfect blend of technology related features in a watch. They are in a race to provide a user-friendly interface in a compact design with high productivity.
