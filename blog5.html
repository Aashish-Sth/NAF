HOW TECHNOLOGY CAN BE USED TO BOOST SALES OF WATCHES?

The process of selling watch combined with technology is getting popular and is attracting a lot of customers. In recent years with the help of technology, it’s aspects are frequently used to promote and boost sales of watches. The concept of branding and partnership with popular figures, celebrities and content creators.

Watch company such as Jacob & Co, Richard Millie, Rolex, Armani are actively partnering with public figures celebrity for their brand promotion. Such promotion has increased viewership, and sales of these brands. Ecommerce website of watch brands are constantly focusing in boost of sale by providing discounts, deducting shipping cost, clearance, festive sales so that there can be proper management of inventory and they can sell their watches which are not as popular as their top or more preferred watches. They also hire a group of professionals who focus on brand promotion, price segment which helps boost in watch sales. Example, Jacob & CO and CR7 have launched a watch which was extremely popular among football enthusiast this directly helps to increase the sales of Jacob & CO and CR7 has gained high amount of profit through high number of sales. Roger Feder is a well known athlete in the world of Tennis. Roger Feder is the official brand ambassador of Rolex which has helped Rolex to gain popularity among tennis fans. This is also an important example of brand partnership with popular celebrities.

In conclusion use of technology in brand has boosted sales of watch in a significant way. Collaboration with celebrities, popular figures, other brands. Due to their presence and fan following, slowly people have developed a sense of craze in such products. Names like Cristiano Ronaldo, Roger Feder are the ones who had significant impact in their respective fields.
